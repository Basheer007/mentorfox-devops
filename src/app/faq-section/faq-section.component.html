How do multicloud and DevOps work together?

Multicloud environments provide diverse tools and platforms, while DevOps practices ensure that these tools are integrated efficiently into the development and operations processes. DevOps methodologies can streamline and automate workflows across different cloud platforms, enhancing overall efficiency and agility.

 What are the benefits of a multicloud strategy?

Avoiding Vendor Lock-In: Flexibility to switch providers or use multiple services. Optimized Performance: Choosing the best services for specific needs. Improved Resilience: Distributing workloads across multiple providers reduces risk. Cost Efficiency: Using the most cost-effective services and pricing models.
